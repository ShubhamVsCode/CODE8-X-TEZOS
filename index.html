About Us

What is Tezlndia Internship Program?

Tezlndia Internship Program in collaboration with Code8 is a 6-week long internship program aimed at imparting beginners technical prowess in the field of Blockchain starting from (TBD). It will be a mix of both experiential and a theoretical approach in a systemic manner to help students grab a better understanding of the intricacies of Blockchain Development and also to get hands-on experience with the Tezos Platform.

WHO ARE WE?

Tezos India:

Tezos is an open-source platform for assets and applications that can execute peer-to-peer transactions and serve as a platform for deploying smart contracts using the native cryptocurrency of the Tezos blockchain called Tez. Tezos India is dedicated to growing the Tezos Community In India and introducing Tezos to the thousands of budding developers, startups, organizations & corporations as Blockchain and Cryptocurrency become more mainstream. We believe that a great ecosystem is a sum of all its participants. We want to enable people to seamlessly participate in the Tezos ecosystem in India.

Code8:

Code8 is an experience-based learning platform driven by the energy of code and creativity to churn the wheels of innovation. We are cultivating a culture of project creation to solve real-world problems and an ethos for skill mastering.

FAQ

What is the procedure for selection ?
At first you have to fill the application form via the website in your best capacity explaining about your relevant experience. Based upon the application and other details we will shortlist the members for a 1 on 1 screening to learn more about you.
What is the screening round ?
After the selection based upon the application form. Our mentors will have a 1 on 1 interview screening round to learn more about your web2 and web3 domain related knowledge. We'll make a decision based upon your skills, past experience, willingness and other parameters to further shortlist and finalize your application selection part after this.
What is the time commitment that the program requires ?
It will be a 6 weeks program starting from mid of January. It will take around 30-40 hours of your time per week. It's a self paced learning program. Being a student or a full-time employee is not a barrier. You can even complete it early as per your bandwidth, so that you can work more on the mentioned topics on a weekly basis.
How many students can participate in this program ?
As this is the first Internship Program from our end, we will be shortlisting only 50 members as part of the program, who will be assigned to mentors. As there will be less number of participants so more engagement and focus will be put forward by our mentors and team members so as to help you effectively learn more on the mentioned topics.
How much does this program cost?
The program is absolutely FREE of cost.
Who can apply for this program?
This program has been curated for individuals who want to learn about the basics of web3 and Tezos Blockchain. Being a student or a full-time employee is not a barrier.
